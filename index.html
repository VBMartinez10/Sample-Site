About

New Jeans

NewJeans is a 5-member girl group under ADOR and HYBE Labels. The group is composed of five members: Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Hearin, and Hyein. They debuted in July 2022 with their released debut single "Attention" followed by their two other singles "Hype Boy" and "Cookie". Leading their successful debut and gaining popularity in South Korea and overseas.
